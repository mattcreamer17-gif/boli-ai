Our view

Most of what has to happen next for Boli is not AI. It is an orchestration problem.

EffectiveSoft scoped AWS serverless, DynamoDB, RDS, Kubernetes, Auth0, Interactive Brokers integration, five to ten algorithmic strategies, a 10-to-12 person implementation plan. The plan is good. Drawing the right lines before implementation restarts is the difference between shipping in six months and landing here again in 2027.

60%

Traditional code and database plumbing

Account sync with Interactive Brokers, trade state, position management, audit log, user onboarding, investor reporting. Postgres and queue work, not model work.

30%

Rule-based logic, legible by design

Your five to ten strategies are rule-sets dressed as algorithms. They should be legible as rules so LPs and compliance counsel can read them without an ML background.

10%

The AI surface, wherever a learned model earns its seat

Signal generation, interface reasoning, or something adjacent. One layer, earned on merit, priced against the cost of running it. Not the whole brand, just the piece that pays for itself.
